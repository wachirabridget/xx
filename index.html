Our Technology Solution

MawinguOps is developing a comprehensive climate-smart agricultural platform that provides smallholder farmers with the tools and knowledge they need to adapt to changing climate conditions.

  • Localized Weather Data
    Farm-level, location-specific weather forecasts tailored for Machakos County farming conditions.
  • Climate-Smart Advisories
    Actionable, crop-specific advice and alerts based on weather and agronomic best practices.
  • Optimal Planting Schedules
    AI-powered recommendations for planting, fertilizing, and harvesting times based on local conditions.

Key Challenges

Inadequate Weather Information

Farmers lack access to timely, detailed weather forecasts critical for planning and decision-making.

Lack of Climate-Smart Advisories

General weather forecasts are not localized or actionable for crop-specific decisions.

Production Volatility

Maize production fluctuates dramatically — from 3.8M bags (2022) to 4.2M bags (2023).

Rising Food Prices

Maize prices in 2024 remain 60% higher than five-year averages in most markets.

Why Machakos County?

Declining Yields

Significant declining maize yields in Eastern Kenya.

National Maize Acreage

Machakos contributes to national maize production acreage.

Strategic Location

Close to major markets and distribution channels.
